Privacy Policy for InfiniteForge

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to InfiniteForge ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at infiniteforge.tech and utilize our online tools. Our core principle is privacy by design; the majority of our tools operate entirely on your device (client-side), meaning your files and data are not transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

For the vast majority of our tools, including but not limited to PDF compressors, image resizers, OCR tools, and other utilities, we do not collect, store, or access the files you upload or the data contained therein. All processing occurs directly within your web browser. You can verify this by disconnecting from the internet after the page loads; the tools will continue to function normally.

3. Information We May Collect

Although our tools are designed for client-side operation, we may collect limited information to enhance our services and website performance:

  • Usage Data: We may gather anonymized, aggregated data regarding your interactions with our website, such as popular tools, feature usage, and general traffic patterns. This information is collected through standard web analytics services.
  • Technical Data: We may collect technical details, including your browser type, operating system, and IP address, for analytical purposes and to ensure compatibility. This data is anonymized whenever feasible.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We utilize cookies and similar technologies to improve your user experience. These help us remember your preferences and enhance site functionality. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may impact certain features.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

We integrate third-party services to enhance our website and provide advertising. These services may collect information about your online activities across different websites over time:

  • Analytics Services: We employ Google Analytics to analyze visitor interactions with our website, aiding in service improvements. Google Analytics collects data such as visit frequency, pages viewed, and referring sites.
  • Translation Tools: Certain tools, such as PDF translators, may utilize third-party APIs (e.g., LibreTranslate) to process text. Data is transmitted to their servers for translation. We do not control their data practices and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): We use CDNs to deliver libraries (e.g., Tesseract.js for OCR) efficiently to your browser. CDNs may collect technical data in accordance with their own policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Your interactions with them are subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Usage and technical data are typically retained for analytical purposes and are anonymized or aggregated where possible.

6. Data Security

We are dedicated to securing your information. While absolute security cannot be guaranteed, especially for data transmitted to third parties, we implement reasonable measures to protect the limited data we collect. Our client-side architecture minimizes data exposure by processing most operations locally on your device.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your data.
  • Opting out of certain data collection practices.
  • Requesting data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your requests in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
