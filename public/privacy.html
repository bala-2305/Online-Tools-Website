Privacy Policy for InfinityForge.tech

Last Updated: September 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to InfinityForge.tech ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and tools. The core of our philosophy is privacy by design; most of our tools are built to run entirely on your computer (client-side), which means your files and data are not uploaded to our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

For the vast majority of our tools (including the PDF Compressor, Image Resizer, OCR tool, etc.), we do not collect, store, or have any access to the files you upload or the data they contain. The processing happens directly in your web browser. You can verify this by disconnecting your internet connection after the page has loaded; the tools will continue to function.

3. Data We May Collect

While our tools operate client-side, we may collect a limited amount of information to improve our services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal, aggregated data about how you interact with our website, such as which tools are most popular, features used, and general user traffic patterns. This is done through standard web analytics services.
  • Technical Data: We may collect technical information such as your browser type, operating system, and IP address for analytical purposes and to ensure compatibility. This data is anonymized wherever possible.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your computer that help us understand user preferences and improve site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the site.

4. Third-Party Services and Advertising

We use third-party services to enhance our website and provide advertising. These services may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site.
  • PDF Translator: This tool may use a third-party translation API (e.g., LibreTranslate) to process the text you want to translate. The text is sent to their servers for translation. We do not control their data handling, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): We use CDNs to deliver necessary libraries (like Tesseract.js for OCR) to your browser faster. These CDNs may collect technical data as per their own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Your interactions with them are governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. While we cannot guarantee absolute security for data transmitted to third-party services, we take reasonable measures to protect the limited data we collect. The primary security feature of our website is its client-side architecture, which minimizes your data's exposure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
