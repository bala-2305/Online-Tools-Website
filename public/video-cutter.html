Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

We support all common video formats including MP4, WebM, AVI, MOV, and more. Any video that plays in modern browsers should work.

Is there a file size limit?

Videos up to 200MB are supported. For best performance, we recommend videos under 100MB. Larger files may take longer to process.

How accurate is the cutting?

The cutting is frame-accurate. You can set start and end times with precision, and the tool will extract exactly the segment you specify.

Can I preview before cutting?

Yes! Use the Preview Trim button to see exactly how your cut video will look before processing the final version.

Is my video data secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your video never gets uploaded to any server or stored anywhere.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
