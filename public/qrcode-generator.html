What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a type of two-dimensional barcode that can be read using a smartphone camera. It can store various types of information, such as a website URL, plain text, contact information, or Wi-Fi network details. When you scan a QR code, your device will automatically interpret the data, making it a fast and easy way to access digital information in the physical world.
