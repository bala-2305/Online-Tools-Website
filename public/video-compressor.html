Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

We support all common video formats including MP4, WebM, AVI, MOV, and more. Any video that plays in modern browsers should work.

How much can I reduce file size?

Compression depends on the original video and quality settings. Typically you can achieve 30-85% size reduction while maintaining good quality.

Will quality be affected?

Some quality loss is inevitable with compression, but our presets are optimized to maintain good visual quality. You can adjust settings to find the right balance.

Is there a file size limit?

Videos up to 500MB are supported. For best performance, we recommend videos under 200MB. Larger files may take longer to process.

Can I choose the output format?

Currently, videos are compressed to WebM format for optimal compression. We're working on adding more output format options.

Is my video data secure?

Absolutely! All compression happens locally in your browser. Your video never gets uploaded to any server or stored anywhere.
