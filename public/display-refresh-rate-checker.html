How It Works

This tool measures your display's refresh rate using the browser's requestAnimationFrame API, which synchronizes with your monitor's refresh cycle.

  • Refresh Rate: How many times per second your display updates (typically 60Hz, 120Hz, 144Hz, etc.)
  • Frame Time: Time between frames in milliseconds (1000ms / refresh rate)
  • FPS: Frames per second your browser can render (may be limited by refresh rate)
  • Accuracy: Results are most accurate when the browser tab is focused and active

Note: This measures the effective refresh rate as seen by the browser. Actual hardware refresh rates may vary slightly due to browser limitations and system load.
