How to Play

🎯 Goal: Get four discs in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)

🔴 Players: Red goes first, then Yellow takes turns

⬇️ Movement: Click any column to drop your disc to the bottom

🏁 Win Conditions: Four in a row wins the game

🤝 Draw: Game ends when board is full with no winner

🔄 New Game: Click "New Game" to start over

Strategy Tips

🎯 Center Control: Middle columns are most valuable

🛡️ Defense: Block opponent's winning moves

⚡ Offense: Create multiple threats simultaneously

👁️ Vision: Look ahead 2-3 moves

🔄 Traps: Set up winning positions while blocking

🎪 Practice: Each game improves your strategic thinking

Strategic Thinking

Connect Four develops pattern recognition, planning ahead, and defensive strategies.
