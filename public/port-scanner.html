How Port Scanning Works

Port Status Meanings

Open
Port is accepting connections and a service is running
Closed
Port is reachable but no service is listening
Filtered
Port is blocked by firewall or filtering device
Timeout
No response received within timeout period

Security Notice

Port scanning should only be performed on networks and systems you own or have explicit permission to test. Unauthorized port scanning may be illegal and can be considered a security violation.
