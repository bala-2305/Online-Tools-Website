Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF files.

Can I add background music to my slideshow?

Yes! You can upload audio files in MP3, WAV, or OGG format. The audio will loop if it's shorter than your video.

How many images can I include in my slideshow?

You can add up to 50 images to create a slideshow. Each image can have a custom duration from 1-10 seconds.

What video resolutions are available?

Choose from Full HD (1920x1080), HD (1280x720), or SD (854x480) resolutions for your output video.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images and audio files never leave your device.
