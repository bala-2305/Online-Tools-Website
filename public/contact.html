Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize those and respond as quickly as possible.

Absolutely! We welcome bug reports and feature suggestions. Use the contact form above or email us directly at hmahibala@gmail.com with details about your issue or idea.
