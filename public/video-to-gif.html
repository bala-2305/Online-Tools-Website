Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats are supported?

We support all common video formats including MP4, WebM, AVI, MOV, and more. Any video that plays in modern browsers should work.

Is there a file size limit?

Videos up to 100MB are supported. For best performance, we recommend videos under 50MB. Larger files may take longer to process.

How long can my GIF be?

You can create GIFs up to 10 seconds long. For shorter, more engaging GIFs, we recommend 2-5 seconds.

Is my video data secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your video never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any server.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
