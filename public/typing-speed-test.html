How to Use

🎯 Start: Select difficulty level and click "Start Test"

⌨️ Type: Begin typing the displayed text as accurately as possible

📊 Track: Monitor your WPM, accuracy, and progress in real-time

🏁 Finish: Complete the entire passage to see your final results

🔄 Practice: Try different difficulty levels to improve your skills

💾 Records: Your best scores are automatically saved

Difficulty Levels

🟢 Easy: Simple words and short sentences for beginners

🟡 Medium: Common vocabulary with moderate complexity

🔴 Hard: Advanced vocabulary and complex sentence structures

📈 Progress: Start easy and gradually increase difficulty

🎯 Goal: Aim for 40+ WPM with 95%+ accuracy

💪 Challenge: Hard mode for experienced typists

Keyboard Tips

Position: Keep fingers on home row (ASDF JKL;)
Posture: Sit straight, keep wrists elevated
Pace: Focus on accuracy first, speed will follow
