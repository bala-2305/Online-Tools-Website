Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in many countries. It replaced multiple indirect taxes and is designed to create a unified market.

What's the difference between GST inclusive and exclusive?

GST exclusive means the price quoted doesn't include GST - the tax is added separately. GST inclusive means the quoted price already includes the GST amount.

What are the current GST rates in India?

The current GST rates in India are: 0% (essential goods), 5% (essential goods), 12% (standard rate), 18% (standard rate), and 28% (luxury and demerit goods).

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes, our calculator uses standard GST calculation formulas and provides accurate results. However, always verify with official tax authorities for your specific situation.
