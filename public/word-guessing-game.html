How to Play

🎯 Goal: Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries

🟢 Green: Letter is correct and in right position

🟡 Yellow: Letter is correct but wrong position

⚫ Gray: Letter is not in the word

⌨️ Input: Type letters or click keyboard

⏎ Enter: Submit your guess

⌫ Backspace: Delete last letter

Strategy Tips

📝 Start Smart: Use common vowels and consonants

🔍 Process of Elimination: Use feedback to narrow options

🎪 Pattern Recognition: Look for letter combinations

⚡ Word Families: Try related words once you find letters

🧠 Memory: Remember which letters you've tried

🏆 Practice: Play daily to improve your skills
