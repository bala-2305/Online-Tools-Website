How to Play

🎯 Goal: Combine tiles to reach 2048

⬆️⬇️⬅️➡️ Controls: Use arrow keys or swipe to move tiles

🔄 Movement: All tiles slide in the chosen direction

➕ Combining: Matching numbers merge into their sum

🎲 New Tiles: A new 2 or 4 appears after each move

🏁 Game Over: When no moves are possible

Strategy Tips

🎯 Corner Strategy: Keep highest numbers in corners

📈 Build Up: Create a monotonic sequence

🚫 Avoid Chaos: Don't let small numbers scatter

⚡ Plan Ahead: Think 2-3 moves ahead

🎪 Pattern Recognition: Look for merge opportunities

🏆 Practice: Each game improves your strategy
