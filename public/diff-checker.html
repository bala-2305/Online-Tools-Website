How to Use

1. Input Your Texts

Paste your original text in the left box and the modified text in the right box.

2. Choose View Mode

Select unified view for a compact diff or side-by-side for detailed comparison.

3. Compare

Click "Compare Texts" to generate the diff highlighting additions, deletions, and changes.

4. Export Results

Copy the diff to clipboard or download it as a file for sharing or documentation.
